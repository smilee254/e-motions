The Sanctuary Manual

🧠 Not a Doctor

e-motions is an AI-powered peer support sanctuary and a P2P connection hub. Sentinel is a compassionate thinker backed by expert counselling data — but it is not a licensed psychiatrist, doctor, or emergency medical service. Nothing said in this sanctuary replaces professional clinical care.

🆘 SOS Protocol — Immediate Danger

If you are in immediate danger of self-harm, suicide, or are currently experiencing a violent GBV situation, do not wait for a chat response. Use the SOS button in the top-right corner to access verified Kenyan emergency hotlines instantly.

  • SOS Kenya Red Cross 1199 24 / 7 — Mental health & crisis support
  • GBV Gender Violence Recovery Centre 1195 24 / 7 — GBV survivors hotline (free)
  • BEFRI Befrienders Kenya 0800 723 253 Suicide prevention — free, confidential
  • KAPC Kenya Association of Professional Counsellors 0722 178 177 Professional counsellor referral
  • EMERG National Emergency 999 / 112 Police / Ambulance / Fire
  • MTRH Moi Teaching & Referral Hospital (Rift Valley) 053 2033041 Psychiatric services — Eldoret

🔒 Anonymity & Privacy Oath

To keep the sanctuary safe, do not share your real name, phone number, or home address in P2P lobbies. While e-motions is architected for privacy, your safety is a shared responsibility. Violations are flagged by our Safety Shield and reduce your trust score.

🛡️ The Ghost Vibe — P2P Conduct Rules

Hate speech, bullying, sexual harassment, or deliberate triggering of vulnerable peers will result in an immediate and permanent ban from the sanctuary. This is a space for healing, not harm. All conduct is scored in real-time by the Safety Shield.

📍 Geolocation Disclosure

We use your IP address to place you in a regional Safe Zone (e.g., Ruiru, Kiambu). This lookup runs locally on our server using an offline database — no third-party geolocation APIs are called. We do not track your precise GPS coordinates, store your movement history, or share location data with any external party.

⚠️ AI Behaviour Disclosure

Sentinel thinks by processing patterns from millions of expert counselling conversations (CounselChat, MentalChat16K, KAPC guidelines). Despite this, it can occasionally surface inaccurate, incomplete, or contextually unexpected information. Always apply your own judgment — and if something feels wrong, use the SOS resources above.
